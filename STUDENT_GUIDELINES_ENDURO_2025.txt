üö® TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ISSUES
========================================

PROBLEM: Exam won't load or keeps buffering
SOLUTION: 
- Refresh the page once (only before exam starts)
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Switch to mobile hotspot if WiFi is unstable

PROBLEM: Timer is not visible or not counting down
SOLUTION:
- This is usually a display issue - exam is still running
- Try pressing F11 to exit/enter fullscreen
- Continue attempting - timer is running in background
- Submit as soon as you finish

PROBLEM: Image in question not loading
SOLUTION:
- Wait a few seconds for image to load
- Click on the image area to retry loading
- Check your internet connection
- If persistent, mark for review and report after exam

PROBLEM: Can't click "Submit" button
SOLUTION:
- Scroll to top of page
- Look for green "Submit" button in top-right corner
- If still not clickable, try refreshing (your progress should be saved)
- Contact support immediately

PROBLEM: Accidental tab switch or browser close
SOLUTION:
- Immediately return to the exam tab
- DO NOT refresh or close again
- Continue from where you left off
- Complete exam as quickly as possible
- Note: Excessive tab switches may trigger auto-submission
